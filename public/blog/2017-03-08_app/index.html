An Anatomy of the App Store

Yanfei Wu

March 8, 2017


Life might be easier if you are less picky with apps. But you might end up having a cluttered phone screen with a bunch of apps you are never going to use again.

I am not sure if I will have a good solution to this problem…but don’t stop reading. What I can do is to show you the anatomy of today’s iTune app store. What types of apps are there? How are they priced? How are they rated? Can we spot a “bad” app simply by reading the description? What about the reviews? Is it true that good apps are all alike and every bad app is bad in its own way?

Get Data
